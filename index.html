📲Install on your phone

Android (Chrome):

  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  2. Tap Add to Home screen / Install app.
  3. Confirm. An icon appears like a normal app.

iPhone (Safari):

  1. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add. Open it from the home screen.

Once opened, it keeps working offline, and your data stays on this phone.

ℹ️About

Heart Care stores everything locally in this browser. No account, no internet needed after the first open. This tool helps you keep notes — it does not diagnose. For anything concerning, contact a doctor or emergency services.
